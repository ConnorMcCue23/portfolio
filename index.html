Summer Internship

Over the summer, I had the incredible opportunity to intern at the Center for Engineering Excellence and Discovery. Starting in March, I was brought on as a Software Engineer, tasked with designing a project for middle school students to build over the summer. Over the next couple months, I worked closely with the software development team, along with the electrical engineering and industrial designer teams to build our own rendition of a GameBoy, called a CROQuest. The project was developed primarily in C, and features many classic arcade games such as Snake, Pong, and Tetris.
